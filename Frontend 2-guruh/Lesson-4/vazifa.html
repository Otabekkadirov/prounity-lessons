Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on igredients

Castor Sugar

Finely granulated white sugar

Self raising flour

A pre-mixed combination of flour

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the top of the cakee springs
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
