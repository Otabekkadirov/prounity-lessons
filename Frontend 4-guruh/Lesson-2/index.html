Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor sugar
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on Ingredients

Castor Sugar
Finely granulated white sugar
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour

Methods

  1. Preheat the over to 190Â°C
  2. Grease a 20 cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the preheated pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven or until the top of the cake springs
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
